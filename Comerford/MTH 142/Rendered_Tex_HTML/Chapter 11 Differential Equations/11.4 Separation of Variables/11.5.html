Equilibrium Solutions

In the last example, since

A constant solution of this type is called an equilibrium solution (or just equilibrium).
It is an example of a stable equilibrium, which is an equilibrium solution to which nearby solutions with nearby initial conditions converge.
There are also unstable equilibrium solutions: equilibria from which nearby solutions diverge.
